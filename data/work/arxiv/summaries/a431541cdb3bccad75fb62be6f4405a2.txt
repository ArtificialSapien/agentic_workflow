Supporting AI/ML Security Workers through an autoimmuneAdversarial Techniques, Tools, and Common Knowledge (AI/ML ATT&CK) Framework. The paper aims to provide a framework that can be used by AIengineers to transform them into AI/ ML Security Workers. We provide a four-fold de-nition for security measures and measures and security measures for AI/ml Security Workers as well as their role as cyber-curity professionals. We also provide an easy-to-use deci-ion support system to identify the weaknesses, explore viable attacks, and consider appropriate tech-searches.
